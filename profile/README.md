## 🏔️ Welcome to Karakoram Base Camp

**The Digital Summit for High-Altitude Exploration**

We are building the world’s most comprehensive open-source database for the Karakoram Range. From geospatial route data to the oral histories of local high-altitude porters (HAPs), we bridge the gap between technical data and the raw spirit of adventure.

**The Name:** "Karakoram" means "Black Gravel" in Turkic, but to us, it means home.

---

### 🌈 Contribution Guidelines

We believe the best maps are drawn by those who have walked the path. Here is how you can help:

* **Data Validation:** Help us verify route coordinates and weather patterns using satellite imagery or personal GPS logs.
* **Documentation:** Contribute to our "Local Heroes" archive by documenting the stories and lineages of Karakoram guides.
* **Code:** Improve our data visualization tools or help optimize our API for low-bandwidth satellite connections.
* **Translation:** Help us make safety guides available in Urdu, Balti, and other local dialects.

> [!TIP]
> Check out our `CONTRIBUTING.md` for our full "Code of Conduct" and technical standards.
